About Us


Kiplongu's Cafe is a milk-based products and by-products selling cafe. The cafe specializes in selling different varieties of milk-based products such as milkshakes, ice creams, yogurt, and cheese. The cafe is known for its high-quality and fresh dairy products made from locally sourced milk. Kiplongu's Cafe is a popular destination for dairy lovers and people looking for healthy and tasty snacks. In addition to dairy products, the cafe also serves a range of hot and cold beverages, sandwiches, and pastries. The cafe's welcoming atmosphere, friendly staff, and delicious food make it a must-visit place for anyone in the area. It is located at KVDA Plaza, along Eldoret-Kabarnet road
